Privacy Policy

We collect only the information needed to assess and (if engaged) recover your case. That includes the facts of your situation, identity-verification information required by law, and contact details. We do not sell, rent, or share data with marketers.

Submissions through our intake form are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access is restricted to the team members assigned to your case. Logs are retained for legitimate business and regulatory purposes — typically seven years — then permanently deleted.

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data under UK GDPR and equivalent regimes. Email privacy@levelplainpro.com.

Terms of Service

Use of this website does not create a client relationship. A client relationship begins only on signature of an engagement letter — never through the website, email correspondence, or initial call.

Information published on this website is for general guidance and is not a substitute for professional advice. Outcomes described in case studies are anonymized and specific to those cases; nothing on this site is a guarantee of result.

Confidentiality

Confidentiality is permanent. We never reference, publish, or describe a client matter publicly without that client's written consent — and that consent is voluntary, revocable, and rare.

Where we publish anonymized case studies (see Results), the client has reviewed and approved the wording. Where we cannot anonymize sufficiently to protect the client, we don't publish at all.

Disclaimers

Level Plain Pro provides forensic, investigative, and advisory services. Where regulated legal services are required in a jurisdiction, those are delivered by an admitted attorney or solicitor of that jurisdiction — sometimes a Level Plain Pro of-counsel attorney, sometimes an external firm we coordinate with.

Past results do not guarantee future outcomes. Average recovery percentages cited in case studies and on the Results page are historical and case-specific.

Nothing on this site constitutes financial, investment, or tax advice.
